Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

1. Introduction

Tablely ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our restaurant reservation management platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Restaurant name, owner name, email address, and password (hashed)
  • Billing Information: Subscription plan and payment details (processed by Paddle — we do not store card numbers)
  • Usage Data: Dashboard activity, reservation counts, and feature usage
  • Guest Data: Names, phone numbers, and emails of restaurant guests entered by you
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, and access logs for security purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the Tablely platform
  • Process subscription payments via Paddle
  • Send OTP verification emails via Resend
  • Improve and personalize the Service
  • Monitor for security threats and abuse
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send important account and service notifications

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in isolated MongoDB databases — each restaurant has its own separate database. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • bcrypt password hashing
  • JWT-based authentication with expiry
  • HTTPS encryption in transit
  • Security headers (XSS protection, CSRF, etc.)
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Paddle: Payment processing and subscription management. Subject to Paddle's Privacy Policy
  • Resend: Transactional email delivery for OTP verification. Subject to Resend's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude: AI agent for reservation conversations. Guest messages are processed to generate responses
  • MongoDB: Database storage for all platform data

6. Guest Data Ownership

Reservation and guest data (names, contacts, booking history) entered into your Tablely account belongs to you as the restaurant operator. We process this data on your behalf to provide the Service. We do not sell or share guest data with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data is removed within 30 days. Billing records may be retained longer as required by law.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your reservation and guest data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@tablely.app.

9. Cookies

Tablely uses a single session cookie (access_token) for authentication. This cookie is HTTP-only and expires after 30 days. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at privacy@tablely.app.
